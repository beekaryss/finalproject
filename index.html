ABOUT PORSCHE

The company produces luxury sports cars, as well as SUVs. Porsche's production is largely collaborated with Volkswagen. Side by side with participation in motorsport, work is underway to improve the design of the car and its components.

The emblem of the company is a coat of arms containing the following information: black and red stripes and deer horns are elements of the coat of arms of the German state of Württemberg, the capital of which was the city of Stuttgart, and the inscription “PORSCHE” and the prancing stallion in the center of the emblem remind that Stuttgart brand was founded as a horse farm in 950.

The famous German design engineer Ferdinand Porsche founded the company Dr. April 25, 1931 Ing. h.c. F. Porsche GmbH. In 1939, the company's first car was developed - the Porsche 64, which became the progenitor of all future Porsche. Many components from Volkswagen Käfer were used to build this instance.

To date, Porsche has more than 34,000 race victories, making the company the most titled manufacturer in history. Porsche participated in races such as 24 hours of Le Mans, rally, Formula One, Carrera Cup.
